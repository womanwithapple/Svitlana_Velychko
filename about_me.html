Experience Journey

From Passion to Digital Creation

  • I am Svitlana, originally from Mariupol, Ukraine, and since March 18, 2022, I have been living in Hamburg, Germany.
  • Having experience in working with clients and evaluating personnel, I shifted my career to focus on creating clear and informative advertising materials and user interfaces. My interest in digital photography evolved into a desire to become a web designer, which led me to study UI/UX design.
  • I have 2 years of experience in designing eCommerce stores with different teams, creating visual interfaces and user experiences for websites and applications, as well as content for eCommerce projects (product pictures, animations, custom elements, etc.). Additionally, I have worked as a content designer for Social Media Marketing.
  • For the past 2 years, I have been working as a junior art director, collaborating with creative teams to conceptualize and produce online and print advertising materials. I have been involved in creating and developing a user-friendly website for a volunteer organization, applying my user interface design and UX expertise to ensure the website is intuitive and user-friendly, effectively bridging the gap between users and interfaces.
  • Since May 2024, I have been actively seeking new job opportunities while focusing on enhancing my skills as a UI/UX designer and web developer. During this time, I have completed Google's UX Design courses, studied HTML and CSS at ReDi School, and attended German language courses to achieve a B1 level. I remain committed to professional growth and staying up to date with the latest industry trends.
