Experience Journey

From Passion to Digital Creation

  • My name is Svitlana, and I am originally from Mariupol, Ukraine. Since February 2022, I have been residing in Hamburg, Germany.
  • I hold a Master’s degree in Applied Materials Science, where my thesis focused on research, analytics, and designing materials for specific operational conditions. This analytical background, combined with my practical experience as a Client Manager and local government officer in Mariupol, laid the foundation for my ability to organize, evaluate, and communicate effectively.
  • Driven by a growing interest in digital photography and design, I transitioned into the creative field, earning a Junior UI/UX Design degree from Beetroot Academy. Over the past five years, I have built expertise in designing visual interfaces and user experiences, particularly for eCommerce platforms. My work includes collaborating with teams to create website applications and content for eCommerce projects such as product photography, animations, and custom UI elements. Additionally, I contributed as a content designer for social media marketing, ensuring brand consistency and audience engagement.
  • I worked as a Junior Art Director for the last two years, where I conceptualized and produced online and print advertising materials with a creative team. A highlight of my career was developing a user-friendly website for a volunteer organization, and applying UI/UX principles to enhance usability and accessibility.
  • Since May 2024, I have focused on advancing my skills as a UI/UX designer and web developer. I completed Google’s UX Design courses, studied HTML and CSS, and Python at the ReDi School, and am progressing to the B2 level in German. This continuous learning reflects my commitment to staying updated with industry trends and contributing to innovative, user-centred projects.
  • I am passionate about combining analytical thinking with creative design to craft intuitive solutions that connect users with technology seamlessly.
